About Us

After 12 yrs of successful practice Mr. Amit Shah and Sameer Dilli with who were with the Tata design group, decided to set up their own firm to cater to a truly challenging field of interior design.As principal designers their strengths are drawn from varied, yet complimentary backgrounds Welcome to Abhinav Interior Designers & Turnkey solutions. Abhinav Interior Designers is an international interior design company specializing in the design and fit out of corporate offices and retail environments. Headquartered in Pune India, with branches in Mumbai and Goa. We believe that the best design concepts are functional, highly efficient and reflect the character and brief of the client. For us client satisfaction is paramount. We are space planners, interior designers and project managers. We complete over 200 projects each year, ranging from 1000sqft to 10,000sqft.A Interior environments provide a significant, physical interpretation of brand, culture and commercial practice – they are not only design solutions, but business solutions.

Quality interiors


is a collaborative effort. Clients know more about the business and we know more about space management. Hence we work collaboratively to create designs that suits the needs of the business, rather than have it the other way around. For an informed and responsive outcome Abhinav engages the clients in the process and lends its expertise to create that are highly functional and of a higher aesthetic quality. Abhinav’s extensive client base covers the corporate, retail, hospitality and residential sectors.
